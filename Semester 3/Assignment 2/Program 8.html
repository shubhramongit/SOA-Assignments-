• "Thinking, Fast and Slow" by Daniel Kahneman - This book offers deep insights into the two systems of thought that drive our decisions. It’s a game-changer for understanding human psychology and improving decision-making skills.
  • "Coursera" (coursera.org) - An invaluable online platform offering courses from top universities across the globe. It’s fantastic for expanding knowledge in any field of study.
  • "Google Scholar" (scholar.google.com) - This resource provides access to a vast database of academic papers and articles. It's crucial for research and finding credible sources.
  • "The Elements of Style" by Strunk and White - A classic book that is essential for honing writing skills. It covers the basics of grammar, composition, and style.
  • "MIT OpenCourseWare" (ocw.mit.edu) - Offers free course materials from MIT. It's a treasure trove for self-learners and students seeking to deepen their understanding of complex subjects.
